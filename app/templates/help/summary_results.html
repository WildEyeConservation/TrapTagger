Summary Analysis

The Summary analysis provides a concise overview of your data, including the number of clusters, images, and sightings, as well as the earliest and latest dates in your dataset. The summary analysis also provides diversity indices for your selected datasets to give you an overview of the biodiversity of your dataset. A species abundace graph is also provided to give you a visual representation of the relative abundance of each species in your dataset. You can select to normalise your species abundance by selecting the Normalise by Site Effort Days checkbox. Finally the summary analysis provides an in depth summary of your camera traps which includes the number of effort days, the number of data units captured per camera trap as well as a camera trap activity heatmap. The summary results is designed to give you a quick overview of your dataset, species diversity and abundance as well as camera trap activity.

To generate the summary results, simply click the Generate Results button. Please be aware that generating summary results may take some time, especially with large datasets. During this process, avoid navigating away from the page or refreshing it.
