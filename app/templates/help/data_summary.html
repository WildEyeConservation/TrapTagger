Data Summary

The Data Summary table provides you with a summary of how many clusters, images, and sightings are in your data as well as the first and last date of your data. The totals are divided into two categories: Valid sightings and Valid Animal SIghtings. Valid sightings are all sightings that are not static or has a confidence above a certain threshold. Valid Animal Sightings are all sightings that are not static, has a confidence above a certain threshold, and has a species classification.

You can filter your data summary by selecting a date range. Please click the Generate Results button to generate the summary results. Please note the summary results may take a while to generate if you have a large amount of data.
