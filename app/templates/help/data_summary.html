Data Summary

The Data Summary table provides a concise overview of your data, including the number of clusters, images, and sightings, as well as the earliest and latest dates in your dataset. These statistics are categorized into two groups: Valid Sightings and Valid Animal Sightings.

Valid Sightings: This category includes all sightings that meet the criteria of not being static and having a confidence level above a certain threshold.

Valid Animal Sightings: This category encompasses sightings that meet the criteria of not being static, having a confidence level above a certain threshold, and having a species classification.

To generate the summary results, simply click the Generate Results button. Please be aware that generating summary results may take some time, especially with large datasets. During this process, avoid navigating away from the page or refreshing it.
