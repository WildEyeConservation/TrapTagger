Welcome to the individuals page. Here you can view all your individuals that you have identified from all your surveys and annotation sets. You can also launch individual identification across surveys and annotation sets. Select on an indvidual's image to view more information about that individual as well as edit the individual.

You can filter you individuals by the following criteria: surveys and annotation sets, species, tags, sites and date range. To clear all the filters you have selected, click the clear button at the bottom of the filters. Change the order of your individuals by selecting the appropriate option from the drow down menu. Search for individuals by their name in the search bar.

You can also launch individual identification for your individuals by clicking the launch ID button. Your currently active identification jobs will be displayed in the Jobs section. You can open an identification job by navigating to the jobs page. You can also stop a indentification job by clicking the stop button after it has finished preparing.
