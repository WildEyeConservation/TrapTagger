Welcome to the individuals page. Here you can view all your individuals that you have identified from all your surveys and annotation sets. You can also launch individual identification accross surveys and annotation sets. Select on an indvidual's image to view more information about that individual as well as edit the individual.

You can filter you individuals by the following criteria:

  • Surveys and annotaion sets: You can select multiple surveys and annotation sets to filter your individuals by.
  • Species: You select which species you want to filter your individuals by.
  • Tags: You can select to filter you individuals by all individuals with tags or for a specific tag.
  • Sites: You can select which sites you want to filter your individuals by.
  • Date range: You can select a date range to filter your individuals by.
You can also clear all the filters you have selected by clicking the clear filters button. Change the order of your individuals by selecting the appropriate option from the drow down menu. Search for individuals by their name by typing in the search bar.

You can also launch individual identification for your individuals by clicking the Launch ID button. Your currently active identification jobs will be displayed in the Jobs section. You can open an identification job by navigating to the jobs page. You can also stop a indentification job by clicking the stop button.
