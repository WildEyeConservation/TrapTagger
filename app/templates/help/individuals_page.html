Welcome to the individuals page. Here you can view all your individuals that you have identified from all your surveys and annotation sets. Select on an indvidual's image to view more information about that individual as well as edit the individual.

You can filter you individuals by the following criteria: survey areas, surveys and annotation sets, species, tags, sites and date range. To clear all the filters you have selected, click the clear button at the bottom of the filters. Change the order of your individuals by selecting the appropriate option from the drow down menu. Search for individuals by their name in the search bar.

You can delete individuals by selecting the delete individuals button. This will allow you to specify the species and annotation set you want to delete individuals from. Please note that this action is irreversible and will permanently delete the individuals from the database.
