Camera Trap Effort Days

The Effort Days Summary serves to calculate the number of days that each camera trap was active during a specific period. This count is derived from identifying the unique days on which images were captured by each camera trap.

This summary is particularly helpful for gaining insights into camera trap activity patterns. You can use it to determine:

  • The active duration of each camera trap.
  • Which camera traps were operational during a specific time frame.

You can categorize the camera traps as either a site, camera, or site group for tailored analysis.

To generate the summary results, simply click the Generate Results button. Please note that generating the summary results may take some time, especially with a large dataset. While waiting, avoid navigating away from the page or refreshing it.

You can further customize the chart's appearance using the Chart Options feature and export it as a PNG image by clicking the Export button.
