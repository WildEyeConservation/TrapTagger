State Space Results

The state space results is a graph that plots the state space used in the spatial capture-recapture analysis. On the graph is the site locations presented as black dots. The grey dots represent the pixels in the state space. The red S's indicate the average spatial locations of detected individuals. The lines indicate if individuals where detected at more than one site and connects the sites where the individual was detected. The graph can be exported as an image by selecting the Export button.
