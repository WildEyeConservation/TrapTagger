Time Analysis

The Time Analysis chart provides a visual representation of species counts over time. This analysis allows you to observe how species sightings change and fluctuate throughout a selected time period. You can choose to display species counts over a defined number of days, months, or years.

Key features of the Time Analysis include:

  • Date Range: Select a specific time period for analyzing species counts.
  • Data Unit: Choose the data unit (clusters, images, sightings, or time to independence) for your analysis.
  • Time Unit: Select the time unit as x days, months, or years.
  • Data: Select the species and site combinations for your analysis.
  • Trendline: Add a trendline to visualize the overall trend in species counts.

The chart generated in this analysis can be downloaded as a PNG image by clicking the Export button. You can further customize the chart display in the Chart Options settings. Additionally, you can access more detailed information by hovering over the data points on the chart.
