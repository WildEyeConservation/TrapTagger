Camera Trap Data Unit Counts

The Data Unit Counts Summary provides an overview of the number of data units captured by each camera trap. Data units can be defined as clusters, images, sightings, or x time to independence, depending on your analysis needs.

This summary is particularly valuable for assessing the total data unit counts per camera trap and gaining insights into the data distribution. Additionally, camera traps can be categorized as either a site, camera, or site group for customized analysis and visualization.

You can download the chart as a PNG image by clicking the Export button. Customize the chart's appearance in Chart Options according to your preferences for export.
