Camera Trap Active Days

The Active Days Chart is a heatmap that provides a visual representation of the number of active days for each camera trap over time. This chart helps you understand when the camera trap was active and how many images it captured on specific days. It also offers interactivity, allowing you to zoom in on specific time periods and hover over individual days to see detailed information.

You can further refine your analysis by specifying a date range filter to focus on a particular period of interest. Additionally, you have the flexibility to categorize camera traps as either a site, camera, or site group for more granular insights. You can also choose to view data for a particular site or site groups. You can filter by a site or combination of sites for further refinement.

To use the Active Days Chart effectively, keep the following points in mind:

  • Generate Results: Click the Generate Results button to generate a summary of the data.
  • Export Chart: You can download the chart as a PNG image by clicking the Export button. Customize the chart's appearance in Chart Options to suit your preferences for export.

Please note that generating summary results may take some time, especially if you have a large dataset. Do not navigate away from the page or refresh the page until the results have been generated.
