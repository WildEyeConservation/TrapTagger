Capture-Recapture Results

Capture-recapture analysis provides abundance estimates for a species using capture mark recapture principles. The results table displays these estimates for multiple models. It's important to note that capture-recapture is performed at the individual level and doesn't rely on spatial information for calculation.

Key aspects of the capture-recapture analysis include:

  • Observation Window: The observation window, specified in days, represents the duration of a sampling occasion.
  • Species: You can apply the analysis to the selected species or all species in the dataset.
