To include specific sites in your group, follow these steps:

  1. Select Polygon: Click on the Polygon button located on the left side of the map.
  2. Draw Area: Use the drawing tool to create one or more areas around the sites you want to include. You can draw multiple areas on the map.
  3. Edit or Delete: On the left side of the map, you'll find buttons that allow you to edit or delete the areas you've drawn if needed.
  4. Save: Once you've defined the desired areas, press the Save button to confirm your selection and save the included sites.
