Species Abundance

The Species Abundance summary displays the total counts for each species in your dataset. You can specify the data unit as clusters, images, sightings, or x time to independence. This summary is valuable for identifying the most common species in your dataset.

To generate the summary results, simply click the Generate Results button. The resulting chart can be downloaded in PNG format by clicking the Export button. You can customize the chart's display using the Chart Options feature to suit your preferences. Keep in mind that generating the summary results may take some time, especially with large datasets.
