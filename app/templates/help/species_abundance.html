Species Abundance

The species abundances showcases the total number of counts for each species in your data. The data unit can be specified as clusters, images, sightings, or x time to independance. This summary is useful for determining which species are most common in your data. A date range can be specified to limit the data used in the analysis.

Please click the Generate Results button to generate the summary results. The chart can be downloaded as a PNG by clicking the Export button. The chart display can be modified in Chart Options to how you would like it do be displayed when exported. Please note the summary results may take a while to generate if you have a large amount of data.
