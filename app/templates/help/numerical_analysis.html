Numerical Analysis

The Numerical Analysis feature provides valuable insights into species counts on a site-level basis, enabling you to gain a comprehensive understanding of your species data.

Here's what you can do with Numerical Analysis:

  • Data Unit Selection: You can specify the data unit for analysis, choosing from cluster, image, sightings, or a custom x time to independence level. This flexibility allows you to tailor the analysis to your specific needs.
  • Comparison: You can compare the total counts of each species or compare on a site-level basis.
  • Species Selection: Select one or more species for analysis, providing a comprehensive view of species counts for the chosen species.
  • Relative Abundance Index: Optionally, normalise the counts by site effort days to calculate the Relative Abundance Index (RAI). This allows you to compare species distribution across sites with different operational periods.

Once you've configured your analysis, you can export the chart as a PNG image by clicking the Export button. Customize the chart's appearance using Chart Options to ensure it's displayed exactly as you need it. For more detailed information, hover over the chart to access additional insights.
