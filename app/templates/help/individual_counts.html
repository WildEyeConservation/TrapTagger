Indivdiual Counts

The Individual Counts result provides a heatmap displaying the individual counts of a species across different sites. This visualization allows you to quickly assess the distribution and density of individuals within your study area.

To access specific individual counts for a site, hover over the corresponding site marker on the heatmap. This feature provides detailed information about the population at each location.

  • Normalisation: Optionally, normalise the heatmap by trap density across the reserve. This reduces bias in areas with more cameras, providing a more accurate view of individual distribution.
  • Radius Adjustment: Modify the radius parameter to control the size of the area-averaged, helping you reveal different patterns in indivdiual distribution.

You can export the map as an image by selecting the Export button. Please keep in mind that exporting is supported only for maps based on OpenStreetMaps.

The Individual Counts result is a valuable resource for understanding the spatial distribution and concentration of a species, making it an essential tool for ecological and conservation analysis.
