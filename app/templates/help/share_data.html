Here you can share a survey with another organisation. Enter the name of the organisation you would like to share a survey with. Select the survey you would like to share from the dropdown menu and select the level of access you would like to give the organisation. The access levels are as follows:

  • Read: The organisation can view the survey and its results but cannot edit or delete the survey.They cannot create, delete or edit annotation sets or launch annotation jobs.
  • Write: The organisation can view the survey and its results and can edit the survey. They cannot delete the survey. They can create or edit annotation sets and launch annotation jobs. They cannot delete annotation sets or annotation jobs.

The organisation will be sent a notification that you would like to share a survey with them. They will then be able to accept or decline the invitation. After they have accepted the invitation, you will be able to manage their access permissions to the survey as well as remove their access to the survey.
