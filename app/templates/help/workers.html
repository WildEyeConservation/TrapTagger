Welcome to the worker management page. Here you can manage what accounts have permission to annotate your data. You will see all accounts with permission listed here along with a number of statistics for each, including your admin account (although you cannot block youself from annotating your own data).

You can perform three actions here:
  • You can invite more workers to annotate for you using the invite button.
  • You can revoke permission for a worker using the remove button.
  • You view more details about a worker with the details button.

Note that if you have some students/interns/workers that you would like to annotate for you, you must ask them to first sign up for a worker account through the website's login page. Thereafter, can you invite them to annotate for you here.
