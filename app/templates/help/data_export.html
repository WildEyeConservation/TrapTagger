Here you can export your data in formats compatible with other software. If your chosen software is not supported, please contact us so that we can potentially add it to the list. At present, the following export formats are available:

WildBook - Simply select your species of interest, input the correct genus and specific epithet for it, along with your WildBook ID, and download. Note that bulk imports into WildBook are limited to 1000 encounters, and as such your export might be split up accordingly.
