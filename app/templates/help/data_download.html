Here you can download your dataset with all the data contained in the image exif data. You can optionally filter the dataset for specific species of interest, and optionally select to sort the images into folders based on the species contained therein.

Please not that it does take time to generate the dataset based on your custom requirements. You will know that the dataset is ready when the survey enters the ready state. You can then download the dataset directly from your S3 bucket under the download folder using S3Broswer.
