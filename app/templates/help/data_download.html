Here you can download your sorted dataset with all the annotated information contained in the image exif data. You can optionally do the following:
  • Filter the dataset for specific species of interest.
  • Either retain the oiginal folder structue in which you uploaded the data, or collapse the images into a single folder with all site, timestamp and label information in the image name.
  • Have the data sorted into separate folders by species
  • Have the data sorted into separate folders for each individual, where possible.

Please not that it does take time to generate the dataset based on your custom requirements. After the dataset has been generated it will download automatically.
